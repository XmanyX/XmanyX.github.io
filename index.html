Welcome to your GameBus Conquest!

You have been tasked with the objective to take over all the provinces in Prodentia!

How do you take over provinces?

To take over a province, you need to score a certain amount of points

These points are awarded for performing a range of tasks, each with a different worth

The range and amount of tasks and the amount of points that are awarded per task can differ between provinces; some may be very hard to do!

Once you take over a province, you can go to a neighboring province to take it over, up until you have taken over all of Prodentia!
